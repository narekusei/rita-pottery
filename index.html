About

Hi, My name is Rita and I’m a ceramic artist based in Tokyo, Japan.

I started working with clay in 2023 and quickly fell in love with the process.

Creating something with my hands brings me a sense of peace and satisfaction that I can’t find anywhere else.

Aside from ceramics, I also enjoy photography, both digital and film. It helps me notice small, quiet moments of beauty — the same kind I try to express through my pottery.

Thank you for visiting and being part of my creative journey.
